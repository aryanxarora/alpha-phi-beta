UFM Organizations and Community Fair 2020

September 14, 2020

The Alpha Phi Beta Fraternity invites all incoming freshmen students to join this year's online UFM Organizations and Community Fair!

The Alpha Phi Beta Fraternity is an 81-year-strong brotherhood based in the UP College of Law with members from all colleges of UP Diliman. In its long history, the Fraternity has produced many successful professionals, staunch nationalists, dedicated public servants, and faithful leaders of the country. The Fraternity aims to continue its tradition of producing vigorous and wise men who stand on the good side of history by ensuring that its younger members are honed and trained in the Alphan way—a legacy inherited from its esteemed founding brothers.

As part of our way to welcome you, the Fraternity will also have its own Zoom breakout room scheduled on Sept. 22, 2020 from 7PM to 8PM.

To join our Zoom breakout room and see other organizations as well, sign up for the event via this link: bit.ly/OCFSignUp

See you there freshies!
